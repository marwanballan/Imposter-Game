How to Play

1. Setup — Add at least 3 players and pick your word categories.

2. Get your card — Each player privately holds their card to reveal it. Most see the secret word. The imposter sees a hint word instead.

3. Discuss — Going around, each player says ONE word that relates to the secret word (without saying it directly). The imposter must bluff!

4. Vote — After everyone speaks, vote on who you think the imposter is.

5. Reveal — Find out if you caught the imposter!
